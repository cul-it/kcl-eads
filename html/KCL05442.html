Biographical / Historical

Richard G. Dixon was Professor of Law at Georgetown University.
This collection consists of decisions, reports, and related documents of the National War Labor Board. Dating from 1942 to 1945, these materials were collected by Professor Richard G. Dixon of the Georgetown University Law School.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
