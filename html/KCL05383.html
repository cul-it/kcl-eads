Biographical / Historical

At the conclusion of World War II, the Curriculum Laboratory which had been set up at Cornell to develop training material for the war industries was transferred to the New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations, and continued to develop training manuals. These manuals were designed to help the teachers of industrial education in the secondary schools throughout the State.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
