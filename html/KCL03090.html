This collection consists of a transcript of an interview with Judge Mary Donlon on March 10, 1966. The interview was conducted by Alex Hawryluk in Ives Hall at Cornell University.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
