Title:

Language:
Collection material in English
Central and local government reports, pamphlets and papers of the London Central (Unemployed) Body for London relating to the causes and incidence of unemployment in the United Kingdom and its cure including labour exchanges, benefits and relief. The collection is particularly rich in London material.
