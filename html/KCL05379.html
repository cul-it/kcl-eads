This collection consists of tables of unemployment compenstation statistics from the Statistical Department of the Industrial Commission of Wisconsin. The tables are arranged in alphabetical order by keyword.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
