Abstract:
Scrapbook documenting the Knights of Labor boycott of the Fuller and Warren Stove Company of Troy, New York, from 1886 to 1889.
Creator:
Knights of Labor
Quanitities:
0.33 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
This collection consists of a scrapbook documenting the Knights of Labor boycott of the Fuller and Warren Stove Company of Troy, New York, from 1886 to 1889. Included in the scrapbook are newspaper clippings, leaflets, broadsides, and other documents relating to the dispute, as well as a few loose letters and newspaper clippings.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
