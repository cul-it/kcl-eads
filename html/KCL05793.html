Biographical / Historical

Aaron W. Warner was Professor of Economics at Columbia University. Alfred S. Eichner was Assistant Professor of Economics at Columbia University. James J. Healy was Professor of Industrial Relations at Harvard Business School.
This collection consists of one bound copy of "Analysis of Labor-Management Relations Problems in the Off-Shore Operations of the East Coast Maritime Industry," a study prepared for the Maritime Administration of the United States Department of Commerce by Aaron W. Warner and Alfred S. Eichner, with James J. Healy serving as advisor. The study was presented in June 1966.
