This collection consists of a photocopy of 1925 letter from Eugene V. Debs to Sidney Rissman, with a transmittal slip from Jack Sheinkman to Richard Strassberg in 1986.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
