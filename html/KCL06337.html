DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Seyfarth Shaw Collection of Collective Bargaining Agreements, 1941- 2007
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6337
Abstract:
Photocopies of collective bargaining agreements (CBAs). Unions strongly represented include AFSCME, Laborers, Operating Engineers, Police unions, SEIU, and the Teamsters. The geographic focus of the collection is the state of Illinois. Cook County and the Chicago, IL area are well represented, as is Springfield and a few other areas in the southern part of the state.
Creator:
Seyfarth Shaw LLP
Quanitities:
17 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
This collection consists collective bargaining agreements (CBAs). Virtually all the CBAs in the collection are photocopies. Most strongly represented in the collection are AFSCME, Laborers, Operating Engineers, Police unions, SEIU, and the Teamsters. The geographic focus of the collection is the state of Illinois. Cook County and the Chicago, IL area are well represented, as is Springfield and a few other areas in the southern part of the state.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
