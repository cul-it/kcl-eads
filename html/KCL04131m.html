Biographical / Historical

Professor Maurice Frank Neufeld (October 27, 1910 -April 10, 2003) was an American academic, author, union organizer and Army officer. In 1945, he was one of the two founding faculty members of the School of Industrial and Labor Relations at Cornell University, appointed by its founding dean, Irving Ives.
This collection consists of photocopied materials relating Hinda and Maurice Neufeld, compiled and sent to them in 1992. Included are materials relating to their wedding and Maurice Neufeld's professional activities, as well as personal correspondence. This collection also includes the program from Hinda Neufeld's memorial service on May 3, 1992.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
