Title:

Creator:

Quanitities:

Language:
Collection material in English
This collection consists of Charles Rehmus's notes from interviews regarding the Railway Labor Act, conducted in 1984. Interviews were conducted with Robert Brown, Nancy Coppersmith, Chuck Hopkins, Sheldon Kline, David Lee, Kay McMurray, Gail Opperman, Laurette Piculin, Robert Pike, Roland Quinn, Andrew Ripp, Jack Sage, Susan Bianchi-Sand, Joe Smith, David Stowe, Art Brennan, Seth Rosen, John Bradley, Joe Anderson, John Willits, Walter Wallace, Charles Thomson, Tom Tracy, Ben Aaron, Phillip Ross, John Sytesma, Ralph Colliander, Jim Conway, Dick Cosgrave, Bill Donlon, Dana Eischen, Fred Hardin, Robert Harris, Tom Ingles, George Ives, Richard Kasher, John Peterpaul, Helen Witt.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
