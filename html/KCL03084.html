DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Company House Organs and Union Journal Content Study Files, 1955
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
/3084
Abstract:
This collection contains the working papers of a study conducted for the Council for the Advancement of Secondary Education. The purpose of the study was to determine what economic terms a high school student would need to know upon graduation. As Cornell's part, economic terms in 15 company house organs and 15 union journals were counted.
