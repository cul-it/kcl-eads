Biographical / Historical

The George W. Dinsmoor Company manufactured textile boards and paper tubes. It was still in operation in 1968.
Five views of the exterior of the George W. Dinsmoor Company building. The building originally housed Walworth Bros. Inc., a worsted manufacturer that was liquidated in February 1930.
All photographs by Richard Garber, Andover, Massachusetts, taken in July of 1968.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
