Biographical / Historical

Richard Jacobstein graduated from the ILR School in 1956. He served as ILR Student Council President in 1955-1956.
This collection consists of ILR School Class of 1956 memorabilia and biographical information about Richard Jacobstein.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
