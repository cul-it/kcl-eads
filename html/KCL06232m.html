Photocopy of personal wage ledger of Margaret Del Guericio given to Richard Strassberg by the author in May 2007.
Creator:
Del Guericio, Margaret
Quanitities:
1 folders
Language:
Collection material in English
Photocopies of pages from a small notebook (5 in. x 3 in.) kept by Margaret Del Guericio from January 5, 1985 through Mar 10, 1990 when she was working at the Circle Dress Shop, 412 Central Ave. East Orange, NJ 07018.
For each entry she recorded what appears to be a job code for work done and amount earned that day. Each entry also contains a running total of amount earned in the year to date.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
