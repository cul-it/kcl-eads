Biographical / Historical

The Committee of Interns and Residents (CIR) is the largest housestaff union in the United States, representing close to 14,000 interns, residents and fellows in California, Florida, Massachusetts, New Jersey, New York, New Mexico, and Washington, D.C. CIR contracts seek to improve housestaff salaries and working conditions as well as enhance the quality of patient care. CIR was founded in 1957.
