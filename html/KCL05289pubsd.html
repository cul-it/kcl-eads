DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
AoM Publications (digital), 2001-
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5289 PUBS(d)
Abstract:
This collection consists of publications, in born digital format, from the Academy of Management. Includes individual files each issue of several different publications. Files may in in pdf format or Microsoft doc format. For publications in paper format see Collection 5289 PUBS.
Creator:
Academy of Management (AoM)
Quanitities:
1 collection
Language:
Collection material in English
Includes issues for AoM News, Current BPS, Footnotes, GDO Newsletter, HR Division Newsletter, IJTD Newsletter, IMD News, MOC Newsletter, News Blast, ODC Newsletter, Pespectives, RMD Newsletter, SIM Newsletter, and TIM List Serve.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
