Biographical / Historical

The Academy of Management is a professional organization that thrives on member-driven efforts to further the scholarship of management across many categories. With a rich history in volunteerism, many projects have resulted from our members' collaboration and passion for their work.[http://aom.org/about/]
This collection is photographs of members attending Annual Meetings.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
