Biographical / Historical

The Textile Workers Union of America (TWUA) was an union for textile workers originally established through the Congress of Industrial Oranizations in 1939. In 1976 it merged with the Amalgamated Clothing Workers of America and became the Amalgamated Clothing and Textile Workers Union (ACTWU).
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
