Abstract:

Creator:
Warwick Mills
Quanitities:
1 folders
Language:
Collection material in English
Includes photographs of the Warwick Mills building in Centreville, R.I., after the Great Hurricane of 1938. Two exterior views and one interior view of the damage to fourth-story on left-hand end of building. Corner of fourth floor has been opened up to the elements.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.

Item 1: Hurricane damage to exterior of Warwick Mills
 1938
Format: black and white photograph
Scope and Contents
Gelatin silver print. Image depicts the Warwick Mills building in Centreville, R.I., after the Great Hurricane of 1938. Four-story brick building; damage to fourth-story on left-hand end of building. Corner of fourth floor has been opened up to the elements. 21.5 x 16.5 cm.
Box 1 Folder 1
Item 2: Hurricane damage to interior of Warwick Mills
 1938
Format: black and white photograph
Scope and Contents
Gelatin silver print. Image depicts the Warwick Mills building in Centreville, R.I., after the Great Hurricane of 1938. Damage from hurricane evident from inside the top (fourth) story of the building; part of the roof appears to have caved in; bricks are piled by the wall that is open to the outside. 21.5 x 16.5 cm.
Box 1 Folder 1
Item 3: Hurricane damage to Warwick Mills and surrounding area
 1938
Format: black and white photograph
Scope and Contents
Gelatin silver print. Image depicts the Warwick Mills building in Centreville, R.I., after the Great Hurricane of 1938. Fourth floor has been opened up to the elements; uprooted trees in foreground almost obscure the building. 21.5 x 16.5 cm.
