Biographical / Historical

Since 1970 Philip R. Dankert has worked building the print and electronic based collections at Catherwood. His experience acquiring the literature in the field of industrial and labor relations and human resources management is unrivaled in this country. By latest calculation, approximately 60% of the volumes on Catherwoods shelves have been acquired during his stewardship of building the foremost library of its type in North America and one of only three such libraries in the world. Phil retired in 2004.
During the time that these files were assembled Mr. Dankert served as the Assistant to the Librarian and as the Collection Development Librarian.
This collection consist of correspondence, memoranda, reports, and other materials from the files of Philip Dankert. The files pertain to Labor Studies, Baruch College ILR Masters Program, Metropolitan District Office Library, Rochester Extension Office Library, Buffalo Extension Office Library, Capital District Extension Office, and the Neufeld Project.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
