DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Rochester Transit Company Arbitration Files, 1950
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5104
Abstract:
Arbitration hearing before Albert I. Cornsweet, Chairman, Phillip B. Willauer and Hyman G. Gould in Rochester, New York from June 20, 1950 to June 29, 1950. The issues were: 1. Ability to pay. 2. Hourly wage rates. 3. Arbitration. 4. Hours. 5. Extra men. 6. Overtime. 7. Retroactivity.
Creator:
Rochester Transit Company
Quanitities:
1 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Arbitration hearing before Albert I. Cornsweet, Chairman, Phillip B. Willauer and Hyman G. Gould in Rochester, New York from June 20, 1950 to June 29, 1950. The issues were:
1. Ability to pay.
2. Hourly wage rates.
3. Arbitration.
4. Hours.
5. Extra men.
6. Overtime.
7. Retroactivity.
The collection is arranged in four series: Transcripts, Union Exhibits, Company Exhibits, and Award.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
