DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
TWUA Photographs, 1940-1970
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6924 P
Abstract:
Photographs of strikes, union events, and portraits of union officials.
Creator:
Quanitities:
0.44 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Includes photographs of strikes at Boott Mills and Wood Mills. Photographs of union events such as parades, election participation, and conventions. Also includes photographs of a ITGWF (International Textile and Garment Workers Federation) meeting. Also portraits of union officials.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.

Box 1 Folder 1
Item 1: Textile workers on picket line at Boott Mills, Lowell, Mass.
Format: black and white photograph
Scope and Contents
Gelatin silver print, 1947-1948. Image depicts Boott Mill workers on a picket line in Lowell, Mass., probably during the 1947-1948 strike. Both men and women are represented in the picketing. Sign on left reads: "Boott Mills Local 538 On Strike" and sign held by picketer on the far right reads: "No Contract No Work." Sign held by woman picketer at the front of the line probably says No Contract No Work. See item 2 in this folder for a very similar. 25.25 x 20.5 cm.

Format: black and white photograph
Scope and Contents

One sign reads: "Boott Mills Local 538 On Strike." Three others read: "We have been Marching [ ] Weeks Respect our picket line." Others are too blurry to make out. See item 1 in this folder for a very similar view. 25.25 x 20.5 cm.

 1963
Format: black and white photograph
