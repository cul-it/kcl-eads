DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
IWW Graphics, 0000-2999
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5210 G
Abstract:
Pamphlets and graphic stickers from the IWW. Twenty-three stickers or stamps advertising the IWW. Approximately 14 sticker designs, including "Preamble" text in 3 languages and many stickers, red on buff colored paper, with graphics and slogans.
Creator:
Industrial Workers of the World (IWW)

Collection material in English
