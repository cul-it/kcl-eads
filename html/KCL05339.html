Biographical / Historical

Leonard Sandman was the Labor Attache at the Embassy in Seoul, Korea.
This collection consists of three publications on Korea provided by the Labor Attache at the Embassy in Seoul, Leonard Sandman.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
