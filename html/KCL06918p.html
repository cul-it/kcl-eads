Biographical / Historical

The Tilton Textile Corporation , located in New York City, acted as selling agents and textile converters. It owned most of the stock in and acted as selling agent for the Lone Star Cotton Mills Company, El Paso, Texas. Lone Star incorporated in 1924 under the name El Paso Cotton Mills Company; in 1940 it amended its charter and changed its name to Lone Star.
Includes photographs of the opening room, drawing room, warping room, cloth room, boiler room, and machine shop in the El Paso Cotton Mills in the 1930s. Also includes photographs of spinning room, drawing frame, and a loom in the Lone Star Cotton Mills, circa 1945.
