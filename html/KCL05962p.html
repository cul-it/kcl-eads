Biographical / Historical

Kelenson was a union rep for Local 30, United Public Workers-CIO, and for Local 1, United Federal Workers.
This collection consists of six oversize photographs.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
