Abstract:
Letters from Frances Perkins to Frances White. The collection also includes a sheet of U.S. postage stamps commemorating Ms. Perkins, and newspaper clippings concerning her life, career, and death.
Creator:
Perkins, Frances
Quanitities:
0.33 cubic feet
Language:

Biographical / Historical

United States Secretary of Labor, 1933-1945; lecturer at the New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations at Cornell University.
