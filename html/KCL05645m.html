Biographical / Historical

Peter J. Brennan was Secretary of Labor from 1973 to 1975.
Burton S. Borkan was Financial Secretary for the Patrolmen's Benevolent Association of the City of New York.
This collection consists of a letter from Peter J. Brennan to Burton S. Borkan, dated September 7, 1973.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
