DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Phillips Bradley Papers, 1941-1950
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
/3043
Abstract:
Correspondence and pamphlets collected by Mr. Bradley for the Joint Legislative Committee on Industrial and Labor Conditions of the New York State Legislature in connection with the founding of the New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations at Cornell University.
Creator:
Bradley, Phillips
Quanitities:
4 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
