Biographical / Historical

The Drug Store Workers' Section of the Medical Workers' Industrial Leauge was based in New York, New York. It was affiliated with the Trade Union Unity League.
Pamphlet outlining recent developments in the drug industry, working conditions for drug store workers, and the political stance of the Drug Store Workers' Section of the Medical Workers Industrial League, affiliated with the Trade Union Unity League.
