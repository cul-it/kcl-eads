Biographical / Historical

Keith Davis was Professor of Management at Arizona Ustate University, as well as Past President of the Academy of Management, Western Academy of Management, and Sigma Iota Epsilon.
This collection consists of a bibliography of Keith Davis's work, manuscript of Davis's autobiography, and Davis's Administrative Letters issued during his presidency of the Academy of Management.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
