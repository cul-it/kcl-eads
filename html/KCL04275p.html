DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
ILR School Conference Center Group Photographs from Conferences, 1990- 2004
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
/4275 P
Abstract:
Photos from IBEW, CWA, NYSUT and Old International Teams.
Creator:
New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations (NYSSILR). Conference Center.
Quanitities:
0.33 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
This collection consists of photos from IBEW, CWA, NYSUT and Old International Teams. Also included are staff members from the ILR Extension Staff and Faculty, Off Campus College, Labor Summer Schools, and many photos that have no labelling.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
