DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Impartial Umpire Files, 1934-1983
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6057
Abstract:
This file contains the awards of the umpires in those industries where an impartial umpire system has been established. Auto steel and meat packing are included at present, but others will be added.
Creator:
Quanitities:
24 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use
