Biographical / Historical

The Globe Woolen Company was a business in Utica, New York.
This collection consists of an 1892 booklet containing time table and work rules for the Globe Woolen Company.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
