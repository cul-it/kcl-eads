Title:
ILR School Faculty Arbitration Awards, 1948-1967
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
/4298
Abstract:
This is a collection of arbitration awards where the arbitrator was a member of the faculty of the New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations. There are seventy awards ranging in date from 1948 to 1967. The majority of the awards are from three arbitrators: Ralph N. Campbell, Vernon H. Jensen, and Jean T. McKelvey.
