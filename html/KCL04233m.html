Abstract:
Miscellaneous records from the ILR School's Western Regional Office in Buffalo, New York.
Creator:
New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations (NYSSILR). Western Regional Office
Quanitities:
1 folders
Language:
Collection material in English
This collection consists of the publication, "Reflections on Progress at Work," and an announcement of ILR Extension courses in Buffalo, New York, offered in March and April 1952.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
