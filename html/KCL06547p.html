DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Bruno C. Brunelli Photographs, 1948-1960

Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives

6547 P
Abstract:
Convention photographs and photographs of Bruno C. Brunelli.
Creator:
Brunelli, Bruno C.
Quanitities:
0.11 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Collection reflects Brunelli's work with the Textile Workers Union of America, Local 454, at the American Felt Co. in Franklin, Mass. Includes correspondence, 1948-1950; bylaws, 1960; audits, 1954 and 1960; financial reports, 1949-1953, of Local 454; cash books, 1943-1944; cancelled checks, 1942-1958; and check stubs, 1947-1955. Also contains monthly cash report ledgers, 1942-1956; copies of collective bargaining agreements between TWUA and various New England companies, 1950-1970; and documents pertaining to union activities at American Felt.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
