DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
M.E. Sandsberry, Jr. et al. vs Gulf Colorado and Santa Fe Railway Company et al. Records, 1953-1954
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5378
Abstract:
Thirteen employees of the Santa Fe Railroad and the railroad obtained an injunction restraining the unions from striking to secure a union shop contract. The collection consists of company documents as the case moved through various courts as listed below.
Creator:
Sandsberry, M.E., Jr.
Gulf Colorado and Santa Fe Railway Company
Quanitities:
2 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Thirteen employees of the Santa Fe Railroad and the railroad obtained an injunction restraining the unions from striking to secure a union shop contract.
The collection consists of company documents as the case moved through various courts.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
