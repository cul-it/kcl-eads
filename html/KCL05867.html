Biographical / Historical

Kelly was a pilot for United Air Lines and a member of Air Lines Pilot Association Executive Board for many years, in the 1940s and 1950s. He also served on the system board of adjustment.
The papers include cases presented before the system board of adjustment and discussions of health benefits for pilots and stewardesses.
Also included are the papers of H.W. Reid, a member of UAL's Master Executive Council in the early 1950s.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
