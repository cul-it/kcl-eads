DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
S. Stanley Alderfer Arbitration Files, 1946-1978
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5576
Abstract:
Files of the arbitrator Stanley S. Alderfer. Over four hundred decisions are included, many of which are multi-grievance cases.
Creator:
Alderfer, S. Stanley
Quanitities:
2 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Arranged chronologically by the year of the decision. Unless otherwise noted, the file contains only the award.
"A total of about 400 plus decisions are involved. Quite a number are multi-grievance cases, so about 500 grievance decisions are included."-- Stanley S. Alderfer
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
