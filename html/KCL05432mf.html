DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
International Brotherhood of Pulp Sulphite and Paper Mill Workers Records on Microfilm, 1906-1957
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5432 mf
Abstract:
These papers of the IBPSPMW cover most of the years from 1906, the year of the organization of the International Brotherhood, to 1957. Files for a few years are missing.
Creator:
Quanitities:
31.78 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Microfilm copies of the files of the International Brotherhood of Pulp, Sulphite, and Paper Mill Workers.
This collection consists primarily of the President-Secretary's files.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
