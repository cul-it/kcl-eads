DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Sidney Harwood Collection of Textile Industry Graphics, 1908-1910, undated
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6890 G
Abstract:
Engineering drawings and blueprints for textile machinery.
Creator:
Sidney Harwood
American Textile History Museum
Quanitities:
0.11 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English, Spanish
Engineering drawings from James Hunter Machine Company for wool washers (text in Spanish). Engineering drawings from Davis & Furber Machine Company for finisher cards, breaker cards, burr machines, and pickers. Also includes blueprints for carding machines and oilers designed by Fred Spivey.
Custodial History

American Textile History Museum Collection, gift of Sidney Harwood.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
