Biographical / Historical

Echo was a publication of the Filene Co-Operative Association, the company union of the William Filene's Sons Company, an American department store in Boston, Massachusetts.
This collection consists of issues of Echo, the publication of the Filene Co-Operative Association, 1902-1912. It includes 48 single copies of Echo and 1 bound copy.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
