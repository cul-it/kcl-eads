DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Local 1199 Bread and Roses Cultural Project Women of Hope Poster Sets, 1994- 1999
Repository:

Collection Number:
6084 G
Creator:
National Union of Hospital and Nursing Home Employees (NUHHCE)
Local 1199 Drug and Hospital Union
Quanitities:
4.5 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Box 1 contains study guides and brochures for poster sets. The poster sets, folders 2-19, are stored in the map case. Each set consists of 12 18-inch-by-24-inch posters.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
