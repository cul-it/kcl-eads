Biographical / Historical

Alpheus Smith (March 29, 1898 - January 23, 1977) became a professor in the School of Industrial and Labor Relations at Cornell University in 1946. During his time at NYSSILR, he taught courses in human relations, directed the Extension Division, and at the time of the Labor-Management conference in 1959, served as Chairman of the University Seminar Committee. He retired from Cornell University in 1966.
Biographical / Historical

The Labor-Management Seminar entitled "Our Changing Society: Problems and Opportunities for Labor and Management," hosted by Cornell University's New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations (NYSSILR) from October 25-30, 1959, was supported by the Estate of Wilheim Weinberg.
This collection consists of files of Alpheus Smith regarding the the Labor-Management Seminar entitled "Our Changing Society: Problems and Opportunities for Labor and Management," hosted by Cornell University's New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations (NYSSILR) from October 25-30, 1959.
Included are planning documents, announcements, correspondence, and budget materials relating to the seminar.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
