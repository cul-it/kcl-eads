DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Cyril Johnson Woolen Company Photographs, 1920-1962
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6589 P
Abstract:
Photographs of the Cyril Johnson Woolen Company's employees making wool fabric.
Creator:
Cyril Johnson Woolen Company
Quanitities:
0.44 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Consists largely of photographs of employees processing wool and creating cloth. This includes sorting and cleaning wool, spinning, warping, and weaving. There are several photographs of finished cloth being inspected. Also included are exterior views of the plant.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
