DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
New York City Office of Collective Bargaining Records, 1968-1995
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5154
Abstract:
Collection includes: Decisions, Releases, cases, fact-finding reports, Annual reports, impasse panel reports. Divided into 5 series: 1. Releases 2. Decisions from Board of Collective Bargaining. 3. Decisions from Board of Certification 4. Impasses Pane (or Arbitration Board) Decisions 5. Arbitration Cases
Creator:
New York City Office of Collective Bargaining (OCB)
Quanitities:
7 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
