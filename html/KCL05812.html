This collection consists of transcripts of interviews about Perkins by James R. Anderson, with Department of Labor staff Isador Lubin, James E. Dodson, Gerard Reilly and Clara Beyer.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
