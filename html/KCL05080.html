This collection consists of the record of arbitration proceedings between the Chicago & Western Indiana Railroad Company and the Belt Railway Company of Chicago and the Brotherhood of Locomotive Engineers, the Brotherhood of Locomotive Firemen, and the Brotherhood of Railroad Trainmen. The proceedings took place in Chicago, Illinois from September 2 through September 12, 1913.
Witnesses on behalf of the employee were Wallace Combs, Arthur McLinden, Albert C. Smith. Witnesses on behalf of the railroad companies were C.E. Taylor, G.W. Berry, F.W. Urbahns, H.W. Ballou, George G. Hayes, J.L. Nichols, J. Kirk, A.A. Bowman, and E.L. Rupp.
