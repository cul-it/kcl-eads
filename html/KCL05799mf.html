DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Joseph Slight Papers on Microfilm, 1874-1957
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5799 mf
Abstract:
Consists of correspondence, minutes, convention proceedings, and union publications.
Creator:
Slight, Joseph
Quanitities:
1 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Papers include personal correspondence, 1896-1957; minutes of Local Assembly 300 (window glass trades), Knights of Labor, Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania; unpublished history, by Slight of National Window Glass Workers Association of America; correspondence, minutes, ballots, tally sheets, 1901-1924; legal and financial papers, 1889-1921; Minutes of the Allied Building Trades (council) of Rochester, NY, 1932-1954; wage scales and working rules, convention proceedings, pamphlets, periodicals clippings, and membership cards, chiefly of National Window Glass Workers.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use
