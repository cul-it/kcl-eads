Biographical / Historical

Founded in 1913, the National Association of Corporation Schools was a corporate training and consulting group that provided a variety of educational and management development services to businesses, government agencies and individuals. In 1923 it became the American Management Association.
This collection consists of minutes and pamphlets of the National Association of Corporation Schools.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
