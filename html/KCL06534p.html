DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Beattie Manufacturing Photographs, 1950-1975
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6534 P
Abstract:
Photographs of textile machinery, textile workers, and carpet samples.
Creator:
Quanitities:
0.5 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Consists of photographs of machinery, including the process of getting a ring spinning frame into the mill building. There are also photographs of mill workers operating the machinery, raw wool being delivered, and photographs of carpet samples.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.

Item 1: Woolen condenser card
Format: black and white photograph
Scope and Contents
Gelatin silver print, [1945-50?]. Image depicts a woolen condenser card, which is a wool card with a tape condenser on the delivery end. The tape condenser splits the doffer web into numerous narrow strips, condenses them into ropings and winds them on a jack spool. The Beattie Manufacturing Company made carpets. 25.5 x 20.75 cm.
Format: black and white photograph.
