DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
William B. Gould IV Correspondence, 1965-1972
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6412
Abstract:
Correspondence from Mr. Gould's time with the New York law firm of Battle, Fowler, Stokes & Kheel. From 1965 to 1968 Mr. Gould worked there under Theodore W. Kheel, Samuel Pierce, and Morris Lasker.
Creator:
Gould, William B., IV
Battle, Fowler, Stokes & Kheel
Quanitities:
1 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
