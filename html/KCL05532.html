DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Society of Federal Labor Relations Professionals Publications, 1974-1976
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5532
Abstract:
Publications of the Society of Federal Labor Relations Professionals.
Creator:
Society of Federal Labor Relations Professionals
Quanitities:
0.33 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Includes copies of 1974, 1975, and 1976 convention programs, copy of a paper submitted by Wilson Hart for the 1974 convention, copies of papers submitted at the 1975 convention other than those printed in Public Personnel Management. These include remarks by Fibish, Freehling, Chaskelson, Tobias, Reuben and Black.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
