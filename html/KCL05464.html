DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
U.S. Economic Stabilization Program Records, 1971-1972
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5464
Abstract:
This collection consists of correspondence, agendas, policy papers, weekly reports, minutes, Pay board proceedings, and activity reports for the Economic Stabilization Program.
Creator:
U.S. Economic Stabilization Program
Quanitities:
2 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
