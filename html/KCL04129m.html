Language:
Collection material in English
This collection consists of materials relating to a protest against the International Paper Company's recruiting efforts at the ILR School. Included are an information packet for faculty and ILR students, leaflets, and an update on the International Paper Boycott. The protest was organized by Frontlash/Committee on Labor Action.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
