This collection consists of correspondence of the Akron, Canton, and Youngstown Railway Company. Correspondents include the Brotherhood of Railroad Trainmen, National Mediation Board, employees, and prospective employees.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
