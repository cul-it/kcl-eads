This collection consists of court documents from the Pennsylvania Mining Company's case against the United Mine Workers of America, brought before the United States Court of Appeals, Eighth Circuit. The case was first argued in the District Court for the Western District of Arkansas.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
