Language:

The collection contains Hickman Commission Statements; Pennsylvania Governor's Commission Hearings; Minutes of the Commission; and Stenographic Reports of Public Hearings.
Senate Bill no. 355, no. 518, no. 1021 ; House Bill no. 1443 ; Public Employees Relations Bills are all included in this collection.
1. Hickman Commission Statements (sheets)
2. PA Governor's Commission Hearings (tape recordings)
3. Minuets of the Commission (sheets)
4. Stenographic Reports of Public Hearings: Sen. Bill #355, #518, #1021 (originals and mf), House Bill #1443 (originals and mf)-Public Employees Relations Bill.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
