DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Arbitration Board 282 Employees' and Carriers' Exhibits and Interpretations, 1963
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5526
Abstract:
Exhibits, transcripts, and interpretations of Arbitration Board No. 282 in the dispute between the B.L.E. and the Union Railroad.
Creator:
Arbitration Board No. 282.
Quanitities:
9 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
