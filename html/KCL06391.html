DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
William B. Gould IV NLRB Papers, 2004-2001
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6391
Abstract:
The collection consists of correspondence, Congressional hearing files, and some case files from William B. Gould IV's tenure as the chairman of the NLRB.
Creator:
Gould, William B., IV
National Labor Relations Board (NLRB)
Quanitities:
9 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Most of this collection dates from Gould's confirmation as Chairman of the NLRB in 1994 to his resignation in 1998. The collection includes material relating to his confirmation hearing. There are the official Congressional documents as well as documents, news clippings, and letters both opposing and supporting his confirmation. Also included are the NLRB Executive Chronological File, 1994-1998, correspondence with members of Congress, NLRB memoranda, NLRB press releases, and Gould's NLRB Diary. Congressional hearing files and NLRB case files make up a substantial part of the collection. Also significant are the speeches he gave as the Chairman of the NLRB.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
