DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Brotherhood of Railroad Trainmen Additional Records, 1884-1936
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5021
Abstract:
Records of the Brotherhood of Railroad Trainmen, including draft manuscript of a History of the BRT, Convention proceedings, an insurance certificate and a condensed history compiled by S.G. Fleisher.
Creator:
Brotherhood of Railroad Trainmen (BRT)
Quanitities:
1 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Includes folders in chronological order beginning with 1884. Appears to be draft mss of book - "History of the BRT" ; 1st Convention Proceedings, 1884, "oldest insurance certificate" ; "condensed history, 1885-1936" compiled by S.G. Fleisher
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
