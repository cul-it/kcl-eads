Minutes of the Proceedings of a Joint Public Hearing of the Standing Committees of Labor and Industries and Civil Service and Pensions to consider Amendments to Section 108 of the Civil Service Law and the Labor Law, held in the Assembly Chambers, the Capitol, Albany, NY, on April 13, 1965 at 10:00 am.
Members of the Committee present at the proceedings were Senator Edward S. Lentol (Chairman Senate Committee), Assemblyman Frank G. Rosetti (Chairman Assembly Committee), Senator Thomas Laverne, Senator Seymour R. Thaler, Assemblyman Paul J. Curran, Assemblyman Stephan R. Greco, Assemblyman John E. Kingston, and Assemblyman James E. Powers. Also present were counsel to the Senate and Assembly Committees, Paul E. Cheeseman and Philip Zichello.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
