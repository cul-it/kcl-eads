DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Engineers and Scientists of America Records, 1952-1960
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5574
Abstract:
Collection consists of, historical documents, Executive Board, Officer, and financial records, Convention documents, local/member society files, subject files, publications and photographs.
Creator:
Engineers and Scientists of America
Quanitities:
21 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
This collection is arranged by office of origin and alphabetically by subject thereunder.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
