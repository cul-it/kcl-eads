Title:
BLS Collection of Collective Bargaining Agreements (Part 7), 1961- 1968
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6178/007
Abstract:
These are agreements obtained in the July 1, 1965 surveys in the building trades, printing trades, local trucking, and local transit industries.
Creator:
United States. Department of Labor
Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)
