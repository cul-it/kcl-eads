Biographical / Historical

The United Brotherhood of Carpenters and Joiners of America, often referred to as the United Brotherhood of Carpenters was formed in 1881. It has grown to be one of the largest trade unions in the United States, and between its chapters and locals there is international cooperation. Local 125 is based in Utica, New York.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
