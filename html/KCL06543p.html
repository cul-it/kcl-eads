DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Bolta Rubber Company Photographs, 1930-1939
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6543 P
Abstract:
Photographs of employees and machinery in the production of rubber goods.
Creator:
Bolta Rubber Company
Quanitities:
0.33 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Includes images of the buffing room, calendering room, and combing room. There are also several photographs of employees, both inside and outside the factory. Three of the photographs show the demolition of building no.16.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.

Item 3: Employees outside office
 1931-1936
Scope and Contents
Image shows employees outside the office of the Bolta Rubber Company in Lawrence, Mass. The employees are identified (on the reverse) from left, as Bob Kesler, Mr. Houston, Lydia Halbech. The photograph is dated 1931 on the reverse, but Kesler and Halbech do not appear in the Lawrence city directories until 1936, he as a clerk at the company and she as a stenographer. Her clothing also suggests a date later in the '30s. The Bolta Rubber Co. of Lawrence, Mass., manufactured hard rubber goods. 19.5 x 23.5 cm.
Format: Black and White photograph.
