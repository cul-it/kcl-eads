Title:

Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives

5006
Abstract:
Transcripts and exhibits of the United States Emergency Board No. 174, pertaining to the Brotherhood of Locomotive Engineers, the Order of Railway Conductors and Brakemen, and the National Railway Labor Conference.
Creator:
U.S. Emergency Board
Quanitities:
1 cubic feet
Language:
