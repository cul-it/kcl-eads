Biographical / Historical

R.D. Nesmith Company, was a woolen mill in South Berwick, Maine. Company originally known as Nesmith & Tatterson and was a partnership between R.D. Nesmith and John Tatterson.
Includes account book of Nesmith & Tatterson, 1845-1849, listing goods delivered, including raw materials and dyestuffs, as well as goods sent elsewhere for finishing. Also includes ledger, 1845-1851, of payroll and manufacturing accounts; a work and time book, 1845-1848; a weave room time book, 1851-1852, listing workers' names; and weaving records, 1849-1850, listing workers' names, number of pieces, weight, and number of yards.
