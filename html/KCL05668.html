Title:

Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5668
Abstract:
The folders represent a series of seminars that were held with trade union women staff and leaders which led to the First New York Trade Women's Conference in January, 1974. Preceding the formation of the Coalition of Labor Union Women.
Creator:
