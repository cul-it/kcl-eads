Biographical / Historical

The Commission on Industrial Relations was a commission created by the U.S. Congress on August 23, 1912. The commission studied work conditions throughout the industrial United States between 1913 and 1915.
Microfilm copy of the reports in the Library of the Wisconsin State Historical Society and the Shelf list of the Reports of the Commission.
Reports of the Commission and reports of investigations made for the Commission.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
