Biographical / Historical

The Coremakers Union No. 5394 was organized on April 17, 1891 in Auburn, New York.
This collection consists of two volumes of minutes of the Coremakers International Union of America: Local 5394, dating from 1891 to 1903, and Local 10, dating from 1899 to 1903. Also included are two volumes of dues ledgers.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
