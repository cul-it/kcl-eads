Title:

Repository:

Collection Number:
5456
Abstract:
This collection consists of arbitration files and final offer cases relating to police and fire fighters.
Creator:
Bowers, Mollie H.
Quanitities:
2 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Biographical / Historical

Mollie H. Bowers was a research consultant for the International Association of Chiefs of Police.
This collection consists of arbitration files and final offer cases relating to police and fire fighters.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
