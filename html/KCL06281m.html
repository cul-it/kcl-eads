Biographical / Historical

Jesse Olson was born in 1924 in New York City. He was former vice presdient of 1199 SEIU United Healthcare Workers East. He joined the staff of the union in 1959 and retired in 1984. He passed away in 2009.
This collection consists of an obituary for Jesse Olson, printed out from the New York Times website. The obituary was published on January 26, 2009.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
