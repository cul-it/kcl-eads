Biographical / Historical

Jerry Hofman was a proud union man all his working life. Early in his career he was a member of the Amalamated Association of Street and Electric Railway Employes of America, Division 241, in Chicago, IL. Later he was the Financial Secretary for the United Brotherhood of Carpenters and Joiners of America, Local 1089, in Phoenix, AZ.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
