DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Blacks in the Railroad Industry on Microfilm, 1946-1954

Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives

5989 mf
Abstract:
Materials documenting the struggle of black railroad employees.
Creator:
Scholarly Resources Inc.
Quanitities:
0.11 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
This is a compilation of a variety of materials documenting the struggle of black railroad employees against ouster from the industry by the collusive actions of the companies and the unions. The papers have been divided into six sections: 1) Correspondence 2) Unions and Union-related organizations 3) Writings 4) Legal documents 5) Memoranda, and 6) Printed Material.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use
