Processing Information

Former title: "U.S. Congressional Hearings (Senate Library) 41st-73rd Congress. 1869-1934; U.S. Congress. House, 1919-1920. Plumb Plan."
This collection consists of microfiche copies of materials from U.S. Congressional hearings regarding federal ownership of American railroads known as the Plumb Plan. From the Congressional Record, volumes 232 and 233, 66th Congress, 1919-1921.
