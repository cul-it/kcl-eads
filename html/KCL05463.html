DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
U.S. Railroad Boards of Arbitration, Conciliation and Mediation Records, 1916-1967
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5463
Abstract:
This collection consists of several sub-series of transcripts, hearings, and exhibits relating to government mediation and arbitration in the railroad industry.
Creator:
U.S. Railroad Boards of Arbitration, Conciliation and Mediation
Quanitities:
3 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
