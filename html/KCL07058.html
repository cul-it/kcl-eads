DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Stanley L. Aiges Papers, 1973-2019
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
7058
Abstract:
Binders containing written decisions made by Stanley L. Aiges, ILR Alum '58 and recipient of the 1985 Groat Award, over the course of his 50 year career as a self-employed arbitrator.
Creator:
Aiges, Stanley Leonard, 1921-
Quanitities:
10 cubic feet.
This collection consists of the written decisions for cases before independent arbitrator Stanley L. Aiges. The decisions are arranged chronologically, and a guide to all cases may be found in Box 1, Folder 1 "Case Index, 1973 - 2019." Additionally, a series of decisions concerning Mack Trucks, Inc. are foldered seperately in chronological order.
Biographical / Historical

Stanley L. Aiges is a labor-management arbitrator who lives and practices in Fort Lee, NJ. Mr. Aiges graduated from the ILR School, Cornell University in 1958. Mr. Aiges was awarded the Judge William B. Groat Alumni Award in 1985 in recognition of his contributions to the field of arbitration.
