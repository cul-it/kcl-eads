Biographical / Historical

The committee appointed by the National Mediation Board in this dispute was J. Glenn Donaldson (chairman), David H. Stowe, and George S. Ives. They concluded that "On the basis of the entire record, this Committee finds that all flight deck crew members on United Airlines, Inc., in the job classifications of pilot or captain, reserve pilot, co-pilot, and second officer or flight engineer constitute one craft or class for purposes of representation and collective bargaining under the Railway Labor Act and should be voted together on one ballot for the purposes of representation under Section 2, Ninth, of the Railway Labor Act, as amended."
This collection consists of the typscript findings of a committee appointed by the National Mediation Board in a dispute between the Air Line Pilots Association, International and United Air Lines, Incorporated. They submitted their findings on January 17, 1961.
