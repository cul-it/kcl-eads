Language:
Collection material in English
This collection consists of announcements of the New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations at Cornell University from 1945 through 1980, as well as announcements of summer and other special sessions.
Cornell University Announcements provided information of interest to prospective students, and the respective colleges comprising the university also produced announcements with more specific information regarding course offerings and degree requirements.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
