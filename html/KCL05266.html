Biographical / Historical

Primarily a Socialist-oriented reform group.
Processing Information

Pamphlets are mostly arranged alphabetically by author. A few are filed by title. The original arrangement had empty folders for possible future pamphlets. The empty folders were retained in the collection.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
