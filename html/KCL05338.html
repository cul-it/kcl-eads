Title:
Trades Union Congress (Great Britain) Pamphlets, 1924-1967

Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives

5338
Abstract:
Publications of the Trades Union Congress of Great Britain.
Creator:

Quanitities:
0.5 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
This collection consists of publications of the Trades Union Congress of Great Britain. They are arranged alphabetically by topic.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use
