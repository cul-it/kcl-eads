DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
American Labor Party Meeting Flyer, 1938
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5137m
Abstract:
Flyer for an American Labor Party mass meeting on October 19, 1938.
Creator:
American Labor Party
Quanitities:
1 folders
Language:
Collection material in English
This collection consists of a flyer for an American Labor Party mass meeting of the 5th Assembly District in the Bronx, New York, on October 19, 1938. Among the speakers scheduled for the meeting were candidates for office running on the American Labor Party ticket.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
