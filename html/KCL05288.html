Biographical / Historical

Local 670 of the International Brotherhood of Pulp, Sulphite, and Paper Mill Workers was based in Utica, New York.
This collection consists of the minute book of the International Brotherhood of Pulp, Sulphite, and Paper Mill Workers, Local 670 from Utica, N.Y., beginning in February 1956 and ending in February 1968.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
