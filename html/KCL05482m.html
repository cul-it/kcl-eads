DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
ILGWU Local 1 Cloak & Suit Operators Documents, 1899-1918
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5482m
Abstract:
Three documents relating to Harry Brown and his membership in the Cloak and Suit Operators, Local 1 of the International Ladies' Garment Workers' Union.
Creator:
International Ladies' Garment Workers' Union (ILGWU)
Brown, Harry
Quanitities:
0.78 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Included are a letter to Mr. Brown from the Joint Board of the Cloak and Skirt Makers Unions of Greater New York (1916), an Honorable Withdrawal Card from the International Ladies' Garment Workers' Union (1918), and Brown's US citizenship certificate (1899).
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
