Biographical / Historical

Eric Polisar (June 29, 1923 July 31, 1968) was a member of the faculty at Cornell University's New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations.
A graduate of the University of Wisconsin (BA, 1949; MA, 1950), Polisar taught at the New York State Teachers College at New Paltz before joining the Extension Division of the School of Industrial and Labor Relations in 1952. From 1953 to 1961, he worked on the staff of the Amalgamated Clothing Workers of America. He returned to the Cornell faculty in 1961 and remained on the faculty until his death in 1968.
See the Cornell University Memorial Faculty Statement for Professor Eric Polisar: http://hdl.handle.net/1813/18855
