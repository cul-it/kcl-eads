Title:
Retail Store Employees Union Local 345 (Rochester, NY) Photographs, 1964-1981

Collection Number:
5823 P
Abstract:
Retail Store Employees Union Local 345 (Rochester, NY) Photographs including photos from the City of Rochester, Union Label Week, seminar dinners, union parties, picket lines and miscellaneous activities the union has participated in.
Creator:
Retail Store Employees Union. Local 345
