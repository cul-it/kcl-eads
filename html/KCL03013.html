Biographical / Historical

A graduate of Cornell University (PhD, history), Christopher Lindley was Senior Extension Associate at the New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations at Cornell University.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
