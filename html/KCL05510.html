Title:
Local 1199 Records, 1956-1973
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5510

Retail, Wholesale and Department Store Union (RWDSU)
RWDSU Local 1199 Drug and Hospital Union

55 cubic feet
Language:
