Biographical / Historical

Iron Molders' Union of North America, was established in 1859 to represent craftsmen who cast wrought iron metal products. Local No. 107, represents members from the Auburn, New York area.
This is a collection of minute books dated: Feb. 1893-Oct. 1895, Oct. 1895-April 1902, May 1902-May 1905, July 1905-Sept. 1909, Oct. 1909-Jan. 1914, Jun. 1914-Aug. 1919, Sept. 1919-Dec. 1926.
