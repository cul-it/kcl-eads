Title:

Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives

Abstract:
Correspondence generated in the process of building the Current Documents collection at the Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation and Archives.
Creator:
New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations (NYSSILR)
Martin P. Catherwood Library
Labor- Management Documentation Center (LMDC)
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation and Archives
Quanitities:
7 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English, French, German, Dutch
The correspondence is from various management and labor organizations in response to inquries for copies of collective bargaining agreements, personnel policies, and other documents.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
