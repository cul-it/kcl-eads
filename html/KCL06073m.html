This collection consists of United States Postal Service Collective Bargaining Stamps, issued on March 13, 1975. Stamps are affixed to envelopes noting the first day of issue and a sheet produced for the U.S. Postal Service on collective bargaining.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
