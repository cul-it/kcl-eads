Collection Number:
5367
Abstract:
This case deals primarily with the constitutionality of the New Jersey statute providing for compulsory arbitration of public utility labor disputes. The constitutional issues involved were whether the statute invades a field preempted by the Federal Government; does the statute improperly delegate legislative power under vague standards, and is it a denial of due process.
