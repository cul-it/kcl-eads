Biographical / Historical

The United Mine Workers of America, District 15, represents employees of the Colorado fuel and iron company.
The United Mine Workers of America (UMW or UMWA) is a North American labor union best known for representing coal miners. Now the Union also represents truck drivers, health care workers, public employees and manufacturing workers in Canada and the United States.
