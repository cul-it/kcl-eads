DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Strike Clippings, 1934-1946
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5253
Abstract:
This collection consists of clippings about strikes in New York State from national and local papers made by the Albany Clipping Bureau for the School in 1946.
Creator:
Albany Clipping Bureau
Quanitities:
1.5 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Clippings about strikes in New York State from national and local newspapers. This collection consists of clippings about strikes in New York State from national and local newspapers made by the Albany Clipping Bureau for the school in 1946.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
