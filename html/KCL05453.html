DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Buffalo AFL-CIO Council Miscellaneous Documents, 1937-1958
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5453
Abstract:
Records of the Buffalo AFL-CIO Council.
Creator:
Buffalo AFL-CIO Council
Quanitities:
3 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
This collection is arranged in four series: Proceedings, American Labor Party (ALP), Political Action Committees (PAC), and Miscellaneous Documents.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
