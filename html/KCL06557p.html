Biographical / Historical

Riggs & Lombard, Inc. was a Lowell, Massachusetts, company that manufactured, bought and sold textile and other machinery.
Includes photographs of the stock room, wood and steel erection floors, assembly floors, and machine shops. All photographs were taken by Marion Photographers, Lowell, Massachusetts, on January 13, 1942.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
