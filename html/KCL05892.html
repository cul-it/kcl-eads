Biographical / Historical

Auburn (N.Y.) Area Labor Council is part the New York State AFL-CIO which is a federation of 3,000 affiliated public sector, private sector, and building trades unions throughout the state, representing 2.5 million members, retirees and their families
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
