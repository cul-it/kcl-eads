DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Labor Relations Board Files, 1973-1982
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
6103
Abstract:
The labor relations boards files are comprised of documents from federal and state boards of labor relations in the U.S., Puerto Rico, and Canada. They were collected by the Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation and Archives and formerly housed in the Center's Current Documents collections.
Creator:
Quanitities:
15 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Records of decisions from various federal and state labor relations boards.
The agencies represented in this collection include the U.S. Conciliation Service, the U.S. Division of Management Relations Services, the New York Labor Relations Board, the Pennsylvania Workmen's Compensation Board, the Puerto Rico Industrial Relations Board, the Connecticut State Board of Labor Relations, the Massachusetts Labor Relations Comission, the New Jersey Public Employee Relations Commission, the New York City Office of Labor Relations, and the Labour Relations Board of Canada.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
