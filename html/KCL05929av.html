DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Local 1199 Bread and Roses Cultural Project Audio-Visual Materials, 1955-1996
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5929 AV
Abstract:
Primarily a video collection, this includes news footage, film of working conditions and strikes, documentary film about the Bread and Roses Cultural Project, film from several versions of the stage production "Take Care", healthcare crisis, AIDS, and May Day and Labor Day celebrations, among others.
Creator:
National Union of Hospital and Nursing Home Employees (NUHHCE)
Local 1199 Drug and Hospital Union
Quanitities:
13 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Primarily a video collection, this includes news footage, film of working conditions and strikes, documentary film about the Bread and Roses Cultural Project, film from several versions of the stage production "Take Care", healthcare crisis, AIDS, and May Day and Labor Day celebrations, among others.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
