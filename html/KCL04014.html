DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
ILR School Dean's Office Additional Files, 1966-1985
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
/4014
Abstract:
School administration files, building files, extension files, fiscal files, correspondence, legislation files, publication lists and research development files are included in this collection.
Creator:
New York State School of Industrial and Labor Relations (NYSSILR)
Office of the Dean
Moore, David G.
Risley, Robert F.
McKersie, Robert B.
Quanitities:
26.11 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Records are filed according to the Records Retention Schedule codes:
A-1 through A-26: School administrative files.
B: Building files.
CU-1 through CU-63: University files.
E: Extension files.
FS: Fiscal files.
G-1 through G-50: Correspondence.
IN: Institutes.
I: Industrial Education.
L: Library.
Leg.: Legislation files.
M-1 through M-32: Institutes and Conferences.
PR: Projects.
PU: Publications and Distributions.
R: Research Development files.
ST-1 through ST-10: Undergraduate student education files.
ST-19 through ST26: Graduate student education files.
ST-30 through ST-35: Alumni.
ST-39 through ST-51: Schedules and Program Planning.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
