Collection material in English
This collection consists of the official report of proceedings before the Wage and Hour Division of the U.S. Department of Labor, in the matter of: Special Industry Committee Number 16-A for Puerto Rico to investigate conditions and recommend minimum wages for certain industries. The proceedings took place in October and November 1954, in Santurce, Puerto Rico.
This collection does not contain the report of proceedings in its entirety.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
