Naumkeag Steam Cotton Company
Quanitities:
1 folders
Language:
Collection material in English
Includes photographs of the construction of the Naumkeag Steam Cotton Company factory, exterior views of the factory, and one 1924 photograph of a woman in the factory inspecting cloth. All photographs are attributed to N. L. Stebbins, Boston.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.

Item 4: Cloth inspection, Naumkeag Steam Cotton Company
 1924
Format: black and white photograph
Scope and Contents
Gelatin silver print. Image depicts the cloth inspection room in the Naumkeag Steam Cotton Company building in Salem, Mass., built in 1924. Female employees in the foreground are inspecting cloth; more employees, both male and female, are engaged in folding or packaging material in the background. Photo taken Nov. 14, 1924. 24.5 x 20.5 cm.

Item 5: View of Naumkeag Steam Cotton Company complex
 1925
Format: black and white photograph
Scope and Contents
Gelatin silver print. Image depicts a view of the entire Naumkeag Steam Cotton Company complex in Salem, Mass., built in 1924. The entrance is visible in the foreground; weave shed (?) and other manufacturing areas in background. Loaded horse-drawn wagon at freight entrance in foreground. Photo taken Jan. 16, 1925. Copy A. 24.75 x 20.5 cm.
