DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Sara Gamm Collection of Miscellaneous Materials from the New York City Transit Authority, 1969-1970
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5026
Abstract:
This collection consists of collective bargaining agreements between the New York City Transit Authority and a number of employee organizations, including supervisory organizations, rules and regulations (technical operating rules as well as regulations concerning leave and vacations), and other material relative to the personnel and labor relations aspects of the Transit Authority.
Creator:
Gamm, Sara
New York City Transit Authority
Quanitities:
1 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
