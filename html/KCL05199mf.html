Collection material in English
Senate Bill no. 355, Public Employee Relations Bill and Senate Bill no. 518, Senate Bill no. 1021 and House Bill NO. 1443 - Public Employes Relations Bill - May 23, June 11, August 21 & 22, 1969. These Bills support Public Employees rights to organize, form, join or assist in employee organizations and to select representatives for the primary purpose of collective bargaining.
