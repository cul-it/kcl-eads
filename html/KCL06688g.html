Biographical / Historical

The Hamilton Manufacturing Company was a manufacturer of cotton textiles, founded in Lowell, Massachusetts, in 1824.
Three sets of four images each. One drawing of the footprints of the Hamilton Manufacturing Company buildings, one map of the buildings and surrounding streets, and two isometric etchings of the buildings.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
