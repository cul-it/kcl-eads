Biographical / Historical

The United Mineworkers of America (UMWA) was founded in Columbus, Ohio in 1890. In 1967, W.A. "Tony" Boyle was the President of the UMWA.
This collection consists of reports, speeches, and audiorecordings from the United Mineworkers of America convention, 1976.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
