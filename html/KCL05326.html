Biographical / Historical

The U.S. Secretary of Labor appointed Industry Committee #31 to consider the question of minimuim wage rates for the Puerto Rican industries included in Administrative Order #483 issued on May 22, 1957 and hearings were begun on July 8, 1957.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
