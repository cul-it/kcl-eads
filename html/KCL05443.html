DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
Labor Arbitration Opinions and Awards, 1955-1993
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5443
Abstract:
Miscellaneous arbitration papers collected over the years by the University of California, Los Angeles, and donated to the Industrial Labor Relations School in 1970. An index is located in box 40.
Creator:
University of California at Los Angeles
Quanitities:
39.5 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
The folders are labeled Labor Arbitration Opinions and Rewards instead of Awards. Please note that the proper label should be Labor Arbitration Opinions and Awards and not Rewards.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
Conditions Governing Use

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
