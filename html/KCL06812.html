Biographical / Historical

North Star Woolen Mills was founded in 1864 amd had locations in Minneapolis, MN, Lima, OH, and Sanford, ME. Minneapolis operation closed in 1950 and business centered in Lima. In 1956, moved from Lima to Sanford and in 1959, it merged with Maine Spinning Company.
Records include stockholders' ledger, 1941-1959; annual financial statements, 1950-1954; articles of incorporation, as amended by typescript, 1945, 1949, 1951, 1953 (gift of W.G. Northrup, Portsmouth, NH, June 1963); minutes, 1953-1958, of directors' & stockholders' meetings; and correspondence.

Processing Information

The ATHM # references the accession number given to collections by the American Textile History Museum (ATHM). These numbers have been kept and tracked for reseachers looking for former citations. For images, these numbers will also be the digital object identifer if the item was scanned by the ATHM. For this collection, the ATHM accession number was 0022.146. When these numbers appear in the processing note, the box and folder are the numbers that immedialy follow the accession number. The number 0022.146.1.3, would indicate that the material was formerly in Box 1, Folder 3.
