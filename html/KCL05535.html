DESCRIPTIVE SUMMARY

Title:
International Brotherhood of Teamsters Local 99 (Rochester, N.Y.) Records, 1957-1972
Repository:
Kheel Center for Labor-Management Documentation & Archives
Collection Number:
5535
Abstract:
Records of Local 99, the Rochester, New York Cannery Local of the International Brotherhood of Teamsters.
Creator:
International Brotherhood of Teamsters
Quanitities:
8 cubic feet
Language:
Collection material in English
Records of Local 99, the Rochester, New York Cannery Local of the International Brotherhood of Teamsters. Materials are arranged chronologically, and alphabetically by subject thereunder.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.

This collection must be used in keeping with the Kheel Center Information Sheet and Procedures for Document Use.
