This collection consists of four series: professional correspondence, personal correspondence, professional publications: articles, professional publications: monographs, professional publications: reviews, other writing: fiction, other writing: poetry.
Some of the publications with issues included in this collection: Industrial & Labor Relations Review, The Right to Work, Labour Education in the British Caribbean , Daedalus Perspectives on Business, The Journal of Human Resources, Labour Gazette, Symposium, Management in the XXI Century, The Hermes Exchange, The Columbia Journal of World Business, Business Horizons, Columbia Law Review, Industrial News Letter, The Journal of Political Economy, Brooklyn Law Review, The Accounting Review, Business & Society Review, Monthly Labor Review, Industrial & Labor Relations Review.
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
