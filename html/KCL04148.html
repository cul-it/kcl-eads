Biographical / Historical

George Brooks (July 12, 1908 January 27, 2000) was a professor at Cornell University's School of Industrial and Labor Relations.
A graduate of Yale University (BA, 1930) and Brown University (MA, 1932), Brooks worked for the National Mediation Board, National Labor Relations Board, and the International Brotherhood of Pulp, Sulfite, and Paper Mill Workers until he joined the ILR School in 1961. Brooks published in the Industrial and Labor Relations Review, Cornell Law Review, Review of Law and Social Change, and Monthly Labor Review.
See the Cornell University Faculty Memorial Statement for Professor George Brooks: http://ecommons.library.cornell.edu/handle/1813/17900
Access to the collections in the Kheel Center is restricted. Please contact a reference archivist for access to these materials.
